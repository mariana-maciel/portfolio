Gare do Oriente

A Visual System for Navigation

Gare do Oriente is one of Portugal's busiest transport hubs and an architectural landmark by Santiago Calatrava. Despite its iconic design, the station presents major wayfinding challenges due to its scale, unclear signage, and lack of a cohesive visual identity, making navigation difficult for many users.


To address these issues, a flexible visual system was developed from Calatravaâ€™s architectural forms, creating a modern and adaptable identity. A clear wordmark and a vibrant yet functional color palette ensure legibility across complex physical and digital environments, while the system is applied consistently across all solutions to create a coherent and intuitive navigation experience.
