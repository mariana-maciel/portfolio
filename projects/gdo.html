Gare do Oriente

A Visual System for Navigation

Gare do Oriente is a transportation center made with a modern system enabling a railway station, a bus station and a metro station. Constructed to serve the Expo 98 world's fair, it is located in the Parque das Nações, has become one of Lisbon's iconic landmarks. Calatrava crafted an elegant design that merges form and function, blending cutting-edge architecture with the historic essence of Portuguese culture.


As visitors of this space we faced a wayfinding challenge: the station's expansive layout and multiple platforms made it difficult to navigate efficiently, especially for first-time visitors or those unfamiliar with the complex. The absence of a cohesive visual identity and clear signage contributed to confusion, hindering seamless travel.
