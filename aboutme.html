About

Hiiiii! My name is Mariana Maciel and I'm 20 years old. I'm wrapping up my bachelor's degree in Global Design at IADE Faculty of Design, Technology and Communication of Universidade Europeia. Over the past six years, I've had the chance to dive into various design fields, gaining a well-rounded experience. What surprised me most was discovering a passion for animation and storytelling, things I never expected to enjoy but now absolutely love. I also have a huge passion for Graphic Design Despite a few headaches learning Adobe programs (and Blender!), I found my creative rhythm and can't imagine my work without them.

Beyond my love for design, I love spending time with the people I love. I'm a huge fan of live music and enjoy reading, especially romance and fantasy books. I'm also passionate about cinema. Two of my favorite films are Little Women and The Nightmare Before Christmas. I'm always looking to expand my knowledge of art history, constantly inspired by the past to fuel my work today.

Another big part of my life is football. I played for eight years, and I'm a dedicated fan of Sporting Clube de Portugal, there's nothing better than catching a game live!
