about me

Hi, I'm Mariana Maciel, a graphic designer recently graduated in Global Design from IADE. With a focus on graphic and web design, I'm now starting my professional journey, always open to learning and expanding my practice.


Over the past years, I've explored different areas of design, where I unexpectedly found a strong passion for animation and storytelling. I'm especially drawn to narrative-driven projects, and my work is inspired by cinema, art history, and culture. I approach each project with curiosity, sensitivity, and a personal point of view.
