The Project (2023)

This project, created in collaboration with Vogue Portugal, focuses on the design and branding of a unisex shower gel called "Amar a Dois." Targeted at Gen Z consumers, this product reflects Vogue's iconic style and dedication to elegance and modernity. Inspired by themes of love, togetherness, and sensuality, the project explores how intimate self-care can be portrayed in a way that appeals to both men and women. The branding is crafted to be sophisticated and discreet, balancing minimalist aesthetics with subtle yet provocative undertones, creating a product that resonates with young, style-conscious consumers.

Overview

The packaging design was central to the project, with a box crafted to embody a luxurious yet approachable aesthetic. Using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I developed a sleek, high-quality box that incorporates Vogue's refined visual language. The box design uses a combination of elegant typography, modern color palettes, and a tactile finish to evoke a sensory connection. The aim was to ensure that the packaging communicates a premium feel and aligns with Vogue's brand identity, offering consumers an elevated, Vogue-approved self-care experience.
